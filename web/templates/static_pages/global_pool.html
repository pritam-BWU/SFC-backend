Reward Area 3

Global Pool Rewards

A member enters the global pool after completing two direct referrals. Once active, they receive shares from future global pool events until their current plan limit is reached.

Entry: 2 completed direct referrals Admin percentage + business net profit Plan cap controls exit

Pool Event Formula

Total Pool Amount

= paid amount x admin percentage + mandatory business net profit

Both parts are mandatory for the global pool calculation.

1

Member Completes 2 Referrals

The qualification is saved permanently. The user does not need to redo this after upgrade.

2

Pool Event Is Created

A new qualified member, recharge, or upgrade can create a global pool event.

3

Amount Is Divided

The total pool amount is distributed among existing active pool members.

4

Cap Is Checked

If a member reaches their plan cap, they exit the active pool.
